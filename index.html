Our Vision

ChemForge India goes beyond traditional virtual laboratories by combining interactive simulations, intelligent guidance, and visual learning. Our mission is to make chemistry safer, easier to understand, and more engaging for every student.

Key Features

AI Lab Assistant

Students can ask questions about failed experiments. The AI analyses possible issues such as incorrect concentration, temperature or procedure and provides explanations.

Experiment Designer

Students describe what they want to create or test. ChemForge generates suitable chemicals, equipment, safety measures and a step-by-step virtual procedure.

Molecular Visualizer

Understand reactions at the molecular level by observing atoms, bonds and energy changes during experiments.

Smart Error Detection

The system identifies common chemistry misconceptions and explains the correct scientific reasoning behind them.

Predictive Simulation

Students can modify conditions like temperature, concentration and reactants to explore possible outcomes.

Reaction Explorer

Students can search for reactions based on their goals, compare different approaches and test ideas virtually.

Viva Practice

An adaptive viva system asks questions based on student performance and helps improve understanding.

Teacher Dashboard

Teachers can view student progress, common mistakes, learning gaps and experiment activity.
