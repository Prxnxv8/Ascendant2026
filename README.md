## 🚀 Overview

ChemForge India is an AI-powered virtual chemistry laboratory designed to make practical chemistry accessible, safe, and interactive for every student.

Instead of relying solely on physical laboratories, ChemForge enables users to perform virtual experiments, visualize molecular reactions, understand reaction mechanisms, and receive AI-assisted guidance—all through a modern web platform.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🧪 AI-Powered Virtual Chemistry Lab
- ⚛️ Molecular Reaction Visualizer
- 🤖 AI Mentor for Chemistry Concepts
- 🔬 Interactive Experiment Simulator
- 📚 Research & Learning Hub
- ⚖️ Balanced Equation Generator
- 🧠 Smart Reaction Analysis
- 🛡️ Laboratory Safety Guidance
- 📱 Responsive Modern UI

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many schools lack access to fully equipped chemistry laboratories due to high infrastructure costs, safety concerns, and limited resources. This restricts students from gaining practical experience and understanding complex chemical processes.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

ChemForge India bridges this gap by providing a virtual laboratory where students can:

- Conduct chemistry experiments safely
- Visualize molecular interactions
- Learn reaction mechanisms
- Receive AI-assisted explanations
- Explore chemistry interactively

---

## 🌍 Impact

ChemForge India aims to:

- Improve practical chemistry education
- Increase accessibility to laboratory learning
- Reduce dependence on expensive physical lab setups
- Encourage STEM learning through interactive simulations
